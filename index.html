Bored@school

Curing school-caused boredom since 2021!

Bored@school is designed by students, for students, with the simple goal of preventing boredom at school!

School districts hate games; most online websites are blocked by networks or administration apps, with the excuse of "misuse of technology."  Bored@school believes that technology should be used in the way the user deems most fit; and if that means playing games, then that's what the user should use technology for!

We're constantly working on new games to release to our site. Below are our current games; find a game you like and enjoy the lack of boredom!
